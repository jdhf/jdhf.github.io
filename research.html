Search

Hα survey of SFCGs

We derive and describe the first Hα observations and star formation activity of 11 SFCGs. Those groups present the largest physical concentrations of star-formation activity in the Local Universe.

Star-Forming Compact Groups (SFCGs)

We describe a newly identified sample of Star-Forming Compact Groups which presents a combination of properties such as low velocity dispersions (and virial masses), small dynamical time-scales and high star-forming fraction, clearly distinct from compact group samples studied up to now.

Galaxy Populations in the Cluster Phase-Space

We analyze and compare the distribution in the CPPS diagram (radius vs. velocity) of star-forming and passive populations in local clusters with the accreted and non-accreted halo populations from simulations.

Environmental processes in the cluster environment

We identify which environmental processes affect the star-formation activity of nearby cluster galaxy population depending of the cluster regions and galaxy luminosity.
